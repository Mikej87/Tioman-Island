3D2N Tioman Island Snorkeling Package

Itinerary:

Day 1:

– Ferry transfer from Mersing/Tanjung Gemok Jetty and free and easy after check in.

– Arrival to Paya Beach and check in after 2pm (early check in is subject to room availability).

– Free & Easy.

– Dinner.

Day 2:

– After breakfast, proceed for snorkeling tour (9am-1.30pm) or jungle trekking tour (2.30pm to 5.30pm)

– After tour, free and easy.

– Dinner.

Day 3:

– After breakfast, proceed to check out and transfer back to Mersing Jetty/Tanjung Gemok Jetty.

Includes:

– 2 night stay in Paya Beach Resort.

– Returns ferry transfer (Mersing/Tg Gemok – Tioman – Mersing/Tg Gemok).

– 5 meals (2 breakfast, 1 lunch, 2 dinner).

– Welcome drink.

– Complimentary guided snorkeling tour.

– Snorkeling equipment and life jacket.

– Complimentary Yoga by the Sea

– Complimentary Rainforest & Mangrove Swamp Walk

Excludes:

– Marine park fee & Johor national park fee.

– Tourism Tax (for Non Malaysian Guest).

– Any other items that are not mentioned in the “includes” section.

Notes:

– Itinerary and package content is subject to last minute changes due to weather or operational issue.

– Activity stated is provided on complimentary basis, no refund will be made for cancellation of activities due to weather or operational issue.

– Child between 2-11 years old is applied with child rate.

– Child below 2 years old is free of charge.

– Ferry timing is to be based on the ferry schedule released by the ferry company (Bluewater).
