Visit Tioman Island with our best selling packages!

Tioman Island is located off the east coast of Peninsula Malaysia and is known as one of the best islands to go on vacation. It offers the ideal beach location for families, couples, friends and anyone else who is looking to relax by the ocean.

Tioman Best Seller Tour Package Highlights

  • Stay at the best beachfront resorts at the most affordable prices complete with welcome drinks!
  • Enjoy quality time with loved ones by having dinner on Tioman Islands white sandy beaches.
  • Countless activities such as jet skiing, yoga sessions and massages are available at the resort.
  • Take a guided diving expedition with certified and expert guides who know the best places to go scuba diving.
  • During your visit to the local village, enjoy a delicious seafood dinner which is cooked fresh by the locals.
  • Feel free to explore the island and take part in available land activities such as hiking and cycling.
  • Souvenirs are always within a budget when you shop at Tioman Island as the island is a duty-free zone!

*Package contents may vary. Kindly refer to details on individual tour packages.
